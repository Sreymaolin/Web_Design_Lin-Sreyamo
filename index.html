Welcome to PNC Cambodia

Passerelles Numériques Cambodia was the first project to be launched in 2005, in Phnom Penh.
PNC offers two IT training programs in System and Network Administration (SNA)
and Web Programming (WEP), based on a holistic educative approach including technical and soft skills, and a personal development program.

PNC Values

At the heart of our community are strong values that guide our learning and interactions.
We emphasize respect, honesty, responsibility,
and cooperation—helping students not only excel academically but also grow into thoughtful and caring individuals.

Solidarity

Supporting and helping each other as one family.
Building strong teamwork and unity among students, staff, and community.

Trust

Being honest and reliable in words and actions.
Creating confidence between students, staff, and partners.

Respect

Treating everyone with dignity, kindness, and fairness.
Valuing diversity and encouraging positive communication.

Responsibility

Taking ownership of actions, studies, and commitments.
Being accountable for personal growth and contributing to society.
